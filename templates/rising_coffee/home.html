About

Rising Coffee LLC started with a passion for family, faith, and service. Adam and Althea Rising (a father-daughter duo) partnered to build a family business that focuses on serving others and building community through the love of coffee. Their shared sense of adventure and love for diverse cultures and experiences inspired a journey in coffee that began while living in Italy and traveling the world. The business is only just starting, but the vision to serve people from all stations and walks of life with the uniting flavor of coffee endures.

Blog

Adam- Alright coffee fans! This is getting real now. The Aillio Bullet arrived a few days ago (thanks to my wife and family for investing in us!), and the first few seasoning roasts have been done. It is a far cry from the stove-top popcorn kettle method that has produced so many great, and questionable, roasts over the last two years. The available data is overwhelming! I already long for the simplicity of my grill probe stuck in the lid and listening for the first and second cracks while recording splits on a stopwatch app. That said, I am super excited to up my game and continue the process of learning and improving. Our first official foray into the coffee business world, will be at Coffee Fest in Orlando, FL, this November. I am super excited to build new friendships and partners as we jump in with both feet!!

Philosophy

We all have individual values and philosophies in life. In our complicated and often fast-paced lives, it is important that we are upfront with who we are and why we do things. For Rising Coffee, coffee is the product, service is the experience, and our values and faith drive our commitment to you. We believe every person is uniquely made in the image of God, and for us, that means all people deserve to be treated with dignity and respect.
